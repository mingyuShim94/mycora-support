Privacy Policy

Mushroom Identifier - Mycora
Last updated: February 24, 2026

Mycora is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not require an account, and your identification history is stored only on your device.

Overview

Mycora ("the App") is developed by gguggulab. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Data We Collect

1. Photos You Submit

When you use the identification feature, photos you take or select are sent to Google's Gemini AI service for analysis. These images are processed by Google to provide mushroom identification results. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for how Google handles this data.

Photos are transmitted only when you actively tap the identify button. They are not collected or stored by gguggulab.

2. Analytics Data

The App uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics (provided by Google) to collect anonymous usage data and crash reports. This helps us improve app stability and user experience. Data collected may include:

  • App usage patterns (screens visited, features used)
  • Crash logs and error reports
  • Device type, OS version, and app version

This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

3. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and use advertising identifiers to serve relevant ads. You can manage your ad preferences in your device's Settings under Privacy > Apple Advertising. See Google's Advertising Policy for details.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No account or login required
  • No location data
  • No contacts or address book access

Local Storage

Your identification history (results, timestamps, and associated photos) is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is automatically removed when you delete the App. You can clear your history at any time from the Settings screen.

Camera & Photo Library Access

The App requests access to your camera and photo library solely for taking or selecting mushroom photos to identify. These permissions are optional and requested only when needed.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI — for mushroom identification
  • Firebase Analytics — for anonymous usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics — for crash reporting
  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
